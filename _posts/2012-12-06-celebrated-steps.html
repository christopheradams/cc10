--- title: In 2012 Creative Commons celebrated steps towards open licensing layout: post ---

In 2012 Creative Commons celebrated steps towards open licensing taken around the world.

Creative Commons staff and Affiliates celebrated major steps towards open licensing taken by the World Bank, Geoscience Australia, Equal Futures Partnership, Europeana, PeerJ, Glasspockets, 500px, Blackboard Learn, GoodSemester, UNESCO through the Paris 2012 Declaration, the European Commission's explicit recommendation to increase use of Open Educational Resources, major grants to USA Community Colleges (TAACCCT Grantees) to produce open content, the adoption by Udacity of CC licensing, and the adoption of Open Textbooks by the US State of California and the Canadian Province of British Columbia.

Congratulations!
