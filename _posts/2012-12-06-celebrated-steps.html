--- title: In 2012 Creative Commons celebrated steps towards open licensing layout: post ---

In 2012 Creative Commons celebrated steps towards open licensing taken by:

the World Bank; USA Community Colleges (TAACCCT Grantees); Udacity videos; the California Government adopted Open Textbook and the Canadian British Columbia Government soon followed; the UNESCO Paris 2012 Declaration; the European Commission explicit recommendation to increase use of Open Educational Resources; Geoscience Australia; Equal Futures Partnership; Europeana; PeerJ; Figshare; Faculty1000; Glasspockets; 500px; Blackboard Learn; GoodSemester; and many more. Major publishers are opening access to their content, for example Wiley Open Access and SpringerOpen transitioned to CC BY licenses. Pearson have created in partnership with Gooru a platform that integrates OER and Pearson materials.

Congratulations!
