Saylor.org scan open educational resources (OER) repositories to select and remix OER and have created 271 peer reviewed courses. More than 1.1 million visits to 21 college-level Saylor courses on iTunes U generated more than 290,000 subscriptions and 687,000 downloads in 5 months.

Wikipedia more than 23 million articles in 285 languages. It is the 5th most visited website in the world. In September 2012 it counted over 82,000 active editors.

Connexions includes 22,207 open educational resources comprising 21,105 peer reviewed reusable modules within 1,251 collections used by over 2 million people per month.

Open Learning of the Open University 'open content portfolio' includes 662 free educational courses and has had more than 23 million visits. Open University materials on iTunes U have recorded more than 56 million downloads.
