Each item you license under CC will be used 13 times on average.

and there are many outstanding examples (as at Nov. 2012):

PLOS: 50,000 (CC BY) articles, each one used on average 2400 times.

Khan Academy: holds 3305 (CC BY-NC-SA) videos and has delivered 170 million lessons.

Minecraft Wiki: the wiki about the Minecraft game has 2390 articles (CC BY-NC-SA) which have been viewed 44,000 times.
