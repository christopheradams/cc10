--- title: Each item you license under CC will be used 13 times on average layout: post ---

Each item you license under CC will be used on average 13 times per annum.

There are many outstanding examples (as of Nov. 2012):

PLOS

50,000 articles, each one used on average 2400 times.

Khan Academy

Khan Academy holds 3305 videos and has delivered 170 million lessons.

Minecraft Wiki

A wiki about the Minecraft game has 2390 articles that have been viewed 44,000 times.

CC Mixter

CC Mixter holds 33,502 files, of which 62% (18,965) are remixes. For example, the page profile of BBCrew shows he has created 481 remixes and his work has been remixed 73 times.
