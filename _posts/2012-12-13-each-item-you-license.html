Each item you license under CC will be used 13 times on average.

There are many outstanding examples (as of Nov. 2012):

PLOS

50,000 articles, each one used on average 2400 times.

Khan Academy

Khan Academy holds 3305 videos and has delivered 170 million lessons.

Minecraft Wiki

The wiki about the Minecraft game has 2390 articles which have been viewed 44,000 times.
